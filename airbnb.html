Airbnb Cleaning Services Designed to get you more 5-star Reviews

You need a reliable and affordable cleaning service to prepare your AirBnB listing and create a welcoming experience for your guests. You want a cleaning service that is available on demand, is punctual, and knows what guests appreciate most. Wermond's Cleaning is the cleaning company you’ve been searching for.

Our goal at Wermond's Cleaning is to take the work out of preparing your AirBnB listing for each new guest. Your Airbnb rental is only as good as your reputation, and any good Airbnb host knows the importance of cleanliness when listing their homes. That’s why our priority is to deliver high quality cleaning, consistently – to ensure you get a 5-star review from your guests!

Let us worry about making your rental shine, so you can focus on your core business of getting more Airbnb bookings.

Every time we clean, we’ll provide services that include the following:

All Rooms

  • Vacuum all carpets
  • Sweep or/ and vacuum all hard wood floors and damp mop.
  • Remove all cobwebs
  • Hand dust all blinds
  • Damp wipe all baseboards, window sills, shelves, molding and other woodworks.
  • Wipe all doors including finger prints around door knobs and light switch plates as well as front glass door if any.

Kitchen

  • Clean inside and outside of all appliances including stove, refrigerator, oven and dishwasher.
  • Clean and hand wipe outside, inside the kitchen cabinets.
  • Wipe down counter tops and backsplash
  • Vacuum and wash floors including scrubbing where necessary
  • Clean out the food pantry where applicable

Bathrooms

  • Clean and sanitize bathroom counters and fixtures.
  • Clean mirrors and vanity
  • Clean, scrub and disinfect shower, tile, tub, toilet and sinks
  • Sweep and mop the floors
  • Mildew chemically treated
  • Wipe all light fixtures within range of a 2-step ladder

Extra Services

  • Carpet cleaning
  • Window cleaning
  • Blind cleaning
  • Clean out car garages
  • All rooms trash will be removed
  • Our cleaners are fully trained to provide deep cleaning services and if you have any special requirements you can prepare your own task list and handed to the cleaners on arrival.

FAQ

Do I have to be home when you are cleaning?

No, you are not required to be home while our cleaning service is provided. Many of our regular customers leave us an extra copy of their house key, which we safely keep. Arrangements to meet your needs will be made during our initial consultation.

Do I have to provide the cleaning supplies?

No, we provides our own cleaning supplies and equipment.

Is your company insured?

Yes, Wermond's Cleaning is insured and bonded to ensure the greatest protection of your property. We are proud to say we only work with verified cleaners and we use many resources to vet our employees and do very extensive background checks.

How do I pay for my cleaning services?

We accept cash, checks, credit cards and also PayPal. We ask for payment on the day of service. You can leave the cash or check on the kitchen counter for our cleaning service crew leader to pick up in a sealed envelope.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request that you provide us with at least 24 business hours’ notice if you must cancel or reschedule, so that we can make the necessary scheduling adjustments.
